Our Story

Founded with a vision to revolutionize surgical care through innovative technology and uncompromising quality.

Medonix Surgitech Pvt. Ltd., founded in 2025 and headquartered in Rajkot, is a forward-looking company specializing in high-quality Surgitech products. With a mission to be innovative and technology-driven, the company is committed to delivering advanced surgical solutions that enhance healthcare outcomes and support medical professionals with reliable, modern technologies.
